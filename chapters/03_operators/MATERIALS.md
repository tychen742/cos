# Chapter 03 Materials: Operators

## Current Material

- arithmetic, assignment, comparison, logical, and bitwise operator headings;
- modulus and decrement examples.

## Candidate Additions

- precedence examples that show when parentheses improve clarity;
- a boolean expression example used again in conditionals.

## Common Mistakes

- confusing assignment with comparison;
- reading increment or decrement examples without tracking value changes;
- assuming bitwise operators are the same as logical operators.
