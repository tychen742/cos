# Chapter 03 Organization: Operators

## Chapter Goal

Teach expressions readers need before making decisions and loops.

## Learning Outcomes

- Evaluate introductory arithmetic and assignment expressions.
- Compare values and combine boolean conditions.
- Explain modulus and increment or decrement behavior in controlled examples.

## Sequence

1. Arithmetic expressions.
2. Assignment and update operators.
3. Comparisons and logical operators.
4. Short preview of bitwise operators.

## Deliberately Deferred

- bit manipulation applications;
- operator precedence tables beyond useful beginner rules.
