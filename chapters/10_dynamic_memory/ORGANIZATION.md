# Chapter 10 Organization: Dynamic Memory

## Chapter Goal

Move from fixed storage examples to explicit runtime ownership.

## Learning Outcomes

- Allocate and free storage in a small program.
- Check allocation results where needed.
- Explain ownership and lifetime at an introductory level.

## Sequence

1. Why fixed-size examples are not enough.
2. Allocate and free one block.
3. Runtime-sized collection.
4. Resize and cleanup.
5. Common mistakes.

## Deliberately Deferred

- custom allocators;
- deep memory-debugging tooling until needed.
