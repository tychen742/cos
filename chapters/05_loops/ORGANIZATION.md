# Chapter 05 Organization: Loops

## Chapter Goal

Teach repetition before collection traversal becomes central.

## Learning Outcomes

- Choose among introductory `while`, `do while`, and `for` loops.
- Trace a loop counter and stopping condition.
- Read a nested loop example.

## Sequence

1. Repetition and stopping conditions.
2. `while` and `do while`.
3. `for`.
4. Nested loops.
5. Controlled loop exits.

## Systems Lab

Trace repeated work over a small input range and connect loop bounds to control flow, work performed, and the kernels readers will later build over arrays and files.

## Deliberately Deferred

- general array algorithms until Chapter 6.
